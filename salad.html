Samai halwa

Cooking Method

Step 1.Take 1 medium size beetroot without peal and grate it

Step 2.Add 1 ripped Banana Take 3-4 teaspoons of honey

Step 3:Add little ghee and add a pinch of cardamom powder and add cucumber seeds , cashew, almond

Yours healthy Samai halwa is ready without fire
