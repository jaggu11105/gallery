Ragi vazhapoo Vada

Cooking Method

Step1.Add 1 cup chopped vazhapoo in a bowl of water with rock salt for 10-15 minutes

Step2.Drain the water and add 4-5 tablespoons of ragi flour

Step 3.Add 2-3 tablespoons of nuts powder

Step 4. Add a small amount of grated onion, green chilli and ginger

Step 5: Add 2-3teaspoons of curd and add 1/2 teaspoon of cumin and add require salt

Step 6: Add chopped curry leaves and coriander leaves

Step 7: Add little water and mix it into a tight and Vada consistency

Enjoy this health ragi vazhapoo Vada with friends and family.
