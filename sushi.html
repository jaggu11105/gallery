Thennai Kozhukathai

Cooking Method

Step 1: Take 1 cup of Thennai flour and add it in a mixing bowl

Step 2: Take 1/2 cup of jaggery or palm jaggery.

Step 3: Take 1/4 cup or required amount of grated coconut and add 2 tablespoon of nuts powder,mix it well by adding little ghee and add this mixture in a kozhukathai mould

.......Your healthy Thennai Kozhukathai is ready without fire.......
