Chollam laddu

Cooking Method

Step 1: Take 1 cup of chollam flour and add 1/2 cup of groundnut powder in a mixing bowl.

Step 2: Take 1/2 cup of jaggery or palm jaggery.

Step 3: Take 1/4 cup or required amount of grated coconut, add 2 spoon of ghee and make it as small balls.

.......Your healthy chollam laddu is ready without fire.......
