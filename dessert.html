Kambu aval payasam

Cooking Method

Step 1.Take 1/2 cup of kambu aval in normal water and soak for 10-15 min drain the water and put the soaked aval in a bowl with 1/2 ripped banana

Step 2.Take 1/4 cup of jaggery powder or palm jaggery powder

Step 3.Add 1 cup of coconut milk or cow milk

Step 4: Add a pinch of rock salt and cardamom powder , add cashew, almond and dry grapes

Yours healthy kambu aval payasam is ready without fire.
